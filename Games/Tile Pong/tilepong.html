How to Play Tile Pong

Play


Welcome to SkillSwirl's guide on how to play Pong! Pong is a classic arcade game that was first released in the 1970s. The game is played by two players who each control a paddle on opposite sides of the screen. The objective of the game is to hit a ball back and forth between the two paddles, with the goal of making the ball go past your opponent's paddle to score a point.

Rules of the Game:

  • The game is played on a rectangular screen divided into two halves, with each player controlling a paddle on their side of the screen.
  • The ball starts in the center of the screen and is served by one of the players.
  • Players take turns hitting the ball back and forth with their paddles, with the goal of making the ball go past their opponent's paddle to score a point.
  • The first player to score a predetermined number of points (usually 11 or 21) wins the game.
  • If the ball hits the top or bottom of the screen, it bounces off at an angle.
  • If the ball hits the side of the screen, it bounces off at an angle and the player whose paddle the ball hit gets to serve the ball.
  • If a player fails to hit the ball with their paddle and the ball goes past their paddle, their opponent scores a point.

Playing on SkillSwirl:

On SkillSwirl, you can play Tile Pong against an AI. Simply click the "Play" button and control your paddle using the UP and DOWN arrow keys. The player who scores the predetermined number of points(5) first wins!

Remember, the key to winning Pong is to keep your eye on the ball and stay alert. Good luck!
