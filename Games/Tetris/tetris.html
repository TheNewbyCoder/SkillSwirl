How to Play Tetris

Play


Welcome to SkillSwirl's guide on how to play Tetris! Tetris is a classic puzzle game that has been enjoyed by millions of people around the world. The objective of the game is to manipulate different shaped blocks called tetrominoes, which fall from the top of the screen, in order to create a horizontal line of blocks without gaps.

Rules of the Game:

  • The game is played on a rectangular grid called the playfield.
  • Randomly shaped tetrominoes fall from the top of the screen one at a time.
  • The player must rotate and move the tetrominoes to fit them together on the playfield.
  • When a horizontal line of blocks without gaps is created, that line disappears and any blocks above it will fall.
  • The player must keep creating lines to prevent the blocks from reaching the top of the screen, which would end the game.
  • The game ends when the blocks reach the top of the screen.
